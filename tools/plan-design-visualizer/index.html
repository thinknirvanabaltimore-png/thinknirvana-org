Why this design

The Mercer CFA Institute Global Pension Index consistently ranks streamlined, non-elective retirement systems among the world’s strongest. In the 2025 Index, Denmark earns an A grade (3rd of 52) and Australia a B+, while the U.S. sits at C+ (30th) — held back by patchy coverage and complexity. ThinkNirvana brings that same structural simplicity — immediate vesting and low-cost index efficiency — to U.S. employers.
