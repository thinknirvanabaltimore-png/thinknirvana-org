If you have a team

SIMPLE IRA vs. a 401(k)

Most employers with a team are best served by a 401(k): higher limits, flexible (not mandatory) employer contributions, and a vesting schedule to keep good people. Our PEP delivers all of that — pooled, lowest cost, with the compliance and fiduciary work handled for you.

A SIMPLE IRA can be the right call for a very small team that wants the lowest possible admin and accepts a mandatory contribution and lower limits. Once you want higher limits, flexible employer contributions, or a vesting schedule, a 401(k) pulls ahead — and our PEP carries the compliance load a standalone 401(k) would put on you.
