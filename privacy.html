Who we are

ThinkNirvana Foundation, Inc. ("ThinkNirvana," "we," "us," or "our") is a Maryland nonprofit corporation operating as a Pooled Plan Provider under ERISA §413(e). We operate AIPEP401K, a pooled employer 401(k) plan. Technology infrastructure is provided by Nirvana Ledger LLC under license.

This Privacy Policy covers ThinkNirvana Foundation, AIPEP401K, and the websites aipep401k.com and thinknirvana.org.

What we collect

Waitlist and inquiry forms

When you join the AIPEP401K waitlist or contact us, we collect:

  • Name and email address
  • Company name and employee count (if provided)
  • Your role and current retirement plan status (if provided)
  • The page or source you came from

Plan participants (when the plan launches)

For enrolled participants, we collect personal and financial information required by ERISA and IRS regulations, including name, Social Security Number, date of birth, compensation, and contribution elections. This information is encrypted at rest using AES-256 encryption and is never stored in plaintext.

Website usage

We use Google Analytics to understand how visitors use our sites. This includes page views, time on site, and general geographic region. We do not use advertising trackers or sell browsing data.

How we use your information

  • To contact you about your waitlist status and plan availability
  • To send updates about ThinkNirvana Foundation and AIPEP401K (you can unsubscribe at any time)
  • To administer the retirement plan in compliance with ERISA and IRS regulations
  • To improve our websites and services

What we never do

  • We never sell your personal information to any third party
  • We never share your data with advertisers
  • We never use your retirement account data for marketing purposes
  • We never accept payment from fund companies in exchange for fund selection (no revenue sharing)

Data storage and security

Waitlist data is stored in Google Sheets, accessible only to ThinkNirvana Foundation staff. Participant plan data (when the plan launches) is encrypted at rest using AES-256 via pgcrypto and transmitted over TLS 1.3. Bank account connections are verified via Plaid — we never store full account numbers.

Your rights

You have the right to:

  • Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you
  • Ask us to correct inaccurate data
  • Ask us to delete your data (subject to legal retention requirements for plan records)
  • Unsubscribe from communications at any time

To exercise any of these rights, email hello@thinknirvana.org with the subject line "Privacy Request."

Cookies

Our websites use minimal cookies — only those required by Google Analytics for usage measurement. We do not use advertising cookies or cross-site tracking.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy as our services develop. Material changes will be noted at the top of this page with an updated effective date. Continued use of our sites constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

ERISA §413(e) requires pooled plan providers to maintain certain disclosures. This page provides general disclosure information. Plan-specific disclosures are provided to employers and participants through the formal plan enrollment process.

Plan identity

Plan name: AIPEP 401(k) Pooled Employer Plan

Plan type: Pooled Employer Plan (PEP) — Defined Contribution 401(k)

Pooled Plan Provider: ThinkNirvana Foundation, Inc.

ERISA §413(e) registration: In process — registration being completed with the U.S. Department of Labor prior to first employer enrollment

EIN: To be added upon completion of nonprofit registration

Fiduciary roles

  • Named Fiduciary (Investment): ThinkNirvana Foundation, Inc. — responsible for investment selection, monitoring, and the Investment Policy Statement
  • Pooled Plan Provider (3(16) Plan Administrator): ThinkNirvana Foundation, Inc. — responsible for overall plan administration, ERISA compliance, participant notices, and Form 5500 filing
  • Technology platform: Nirvana Ledger LLC — provides AI-powered recordkeeping and compliance engine under license
  • Custodian / Trust vendor: Being finalized — announcement forthcoming before first employer cohort

Investment policy

AIPEP 401(k) does not use a 3(38) investment manager. ThinkNirvana Foundation serves as Named Investment Fiduciary and makes all investment decisions directly, consistent with the plan's Investment Policy Statement (IPS). The IPS is available to participating employers upon request.

The plan offers five Vanguard index funds exclusively:

  • Vanguard Total Stock Market ETF (VTI)
  • Vanguard Total International Stock ETF (VXUS)
  • Vanguard Total Bond Market ETF (BND)
  • Vanguard Federal Money Market Fund (VMFXX)
  • Vanguard Target Date series (VTIVX and series)

No revenue sharing, sub-transfer agency fees, or 12b-1 fees are accepted from any fund company. The plan receives no compensation from fund providers.

Fee disclosure summary

Full fee disclosure is provided to employers and participants through the formal enrollment documents including the 408(b)(2) disclosure and participant fee disclosure notices. A summary:

  • Employer plan administration fee: $0
  • Participant recordkeeping fee: Flat monthly fee deducted from participant accounts — disclosed in enrollment materials
  • Investment expense ratios: 0.03%–0.15% annually (Vanguard institutional rates)
  • Revenue sharing received by plan: $0

Form 5500

As Pooled Plan Provider, ThinkNirvana Foundation files a single master Form 5500 on behalf of all participating employers annually. Individual employers are relieved of their Form 5500 filing obligation upon joining the plan.

Plan documents

The following documents govern plan operations and are available to employers and participants upon request:

  • Plan Document and Summary Plan Description (SPD)
  • Adoption Agreement
  • Investment Policy Statement (IPS)
  • 408(b)(2) Fee Disclosure
  • Participant Fee Disclosure (404(a)(5))
  • ERISA §413(e) Registration confirmation (upon completion)
