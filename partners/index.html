Why a new alliance is needed

The fee problem is simple. So is the fix.

For a typical small-business plan, roughly a third of a participant's lifetime return can be lost to operational drag — the layered cost of running the plan. The advisory model that built those plans has left that cost in place. A new set of trusted fiduciaries, paired with a transparent pooled plan, can take it out.
