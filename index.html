Our Mission

On a mission to transform how America saves for retirement.

The retirement industry is one of the most profitable financial sectors in the world. It is profitable precisely because it is complicated, opaque, and inaccessible to the people who need it most: small business owners and their employees.

"We intend to compete with every layer of the retirement value chain — recordkeepers, state IRAs, investment firms, TPAs, advisory networks — not to put them out of business, but to force the entire industry to treat savers better."

ThinkNirvana is building the infrastructure for a new retirement system: nonprofit, AI-powered, blockchain-native, and participant-first from day one.

The Fee Drain Hidden fees, revenue sharing, and advisor commissions silently consume 30–40% of a worker's lifetime retirement savings. Most participants never know it's happening.
02
The Access Gap 57 million American workers — overwhelmingly employed by small businesses — have zero access to an employer-sponsored retirement plan.
03
The Complexity Trap ERISA compliance, Form 5500 filings, nondiscrimination testing — the administrative burden is a weapon the industry uses to justify its fees.

What We Stand For

Our operating principles.

⚖️

Fiduciary First

We are legally required to act in participants' best interests. No exceptions, no carve-outs, no conflicts. Ever.

🪟

Radical Transparency

Every fee published. Every vendor relationship disclosed. Every investment return reported honestly. Opacity is how this industry profits — we end it.
