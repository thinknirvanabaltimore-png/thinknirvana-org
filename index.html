Our mission

On a mission to transform how America saves for retirement.

Thirty years ago, a 401(k) required a large employer, an expensive TPA, and an advisor who earned commissions on your employees' savings.

We built something different. A pooled employer plan run by a nonprofit. Vanguard index funds only. AI-monitored quarterly. A low monthly participant fee.

"We intend to compete with every layer of the retirement value chain — not to put them out of business, but to force the entire industry to treat savers better."

The hotel housekeeper. The restaurant manager. The dental assistant. The startup engineer. Everyone deserves this.

01

The fee drain

Hidden fees, revenue sharing, and advisor commissions silently consume 30–40% of a worker's lifetime retirement savings. Most participants never know it's happening.

02

The access gap

57 million American workers — overwhelmingly employed by small businesses — have zero access to an employer-sponsored retirement plan.

03

The complexity trap

ERISA compliance, Form 5500 filings, nondiscrimination testing — the administrative burden has historically been used to justify high plan fees.

Operating principles

What we stand for.

⚖️

Fiduciary first

We are legally required to act in participants' best interests. No exceptions, no carve-outs, no conflicts of interest. Ever. It is written into our governing documents.

🪟

Radical transparency

Every fee published. Every vendor relationship disclosed. Every investment return reported honestly. Opacity is how this industry profits — we end it.

🤖

AI-powered, human-accountable

AI and automation replace expensive human intermediaries and pass the savings to participants. Human oversight ensures the AI serves the mission, not the other way around.

🇺🇸

American workers first

Built for American small businesses and their employees. Every decision starts here — because 57 million workers without a retirement plan is a problem we solve at home first.

Jimesh "Jimmy" Thaker

Founder & Executive Director, ThinkNirvana Foundation
Managing Partner, Nirvana Ledger LLC

Jimmy has spent his career at the center of American financial services — building data and analytics capabilities, leading large-scale technology integrations, and working at the strategic heart of the retirement industry.

At Capital One, he spent 14 years in financial services — starting in credit card risk operations and moving into the Integration Management Office during major M&A expansion, where he led hundreds of people, managed multi-million dollar budgets, and helped build Capital One's blueprint for cloud transition.

At OneAmerica, he led Technology and Data functions for the Institutional Markets and Recordkeeping businesses for four years — gaining deep expertise in retirement plan administration, recordkeeping infrastructure, and institutional markets.

At T. Rowe Price, he served as VP of Business Strategy and Data Analysis in Retirement Plan Services — working at the strategic center of one of the industry's largest asset managers, developing a comprehensive view of how retirement plans serve American workers.

That career-long perspective — from technology infrastructure to investment strategy to plan administration — is what ThinkNirvana Foundation and AIPEP401K are built on: a nonprofit-run pooled employer plan that delivers Vanguard index funds to workers who have never had access to them, with full transparency and no conflicts of interest.

Jimmy is based in Olney, Maryland — where he lives with his family.
