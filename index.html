Our mission

On a mission to transform how America saves for retirement.

Thirty years ago, a 401(k) required a large employer, an expensive TPA, and an advisor who earned commissions on your employees' savings.

We built something different. A pooled employer plan run by a nonprofit. Vanguard index funds only. AI-monitored quarterly. $6/month per participant.

"We intend to compete with every layer of the retirement value chain — not to put them out of business, but to force the entire industry to treat savers better."

The hotel housekeeper. The restaurant manager. The dental assistant. The startup engineer. Everyone deserves this.

01

The fee drain

Hidden fees, revenue sharing, and advisor commissions silently consume 30–40% of a worker's lifetime retirement savings. Most participants never know it's happening.

02

The access gap

57 million American workers — overwhelmingly employed by small businesses — have zero access to an employer-sponsored retirement plan.

03

The complexity trap

ERISA compliance, Form 5500 filings, nondiscrimination testing — the administrative burden is a weapon the industry uses to justify its fees.

Operating principles

What we stand for.

Fiduciary first

We are legally required to act in participants' best interests. No exceptions, no carve-outs, no conflicts of interest. Ever. It is written into our governing documents.

🪟

Radical transparency

Every fee published. Every vendor relationship disclosed. Every investment return reported honestly. Opacity is how this industry profits — we end it.

AI and automation replace expensive human intermediaries and pass the savings to participants. Human oversight ensures the AI serves the mission, not the other way around.
